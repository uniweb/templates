## Pricing FAQ

Have questions about our pricing? Find answers below.

### Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the credit will be applied to your next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, our Professional plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.

### What happens when my trial ends?

You'll be notified before your trial ends. If you don't upgrade, your account will automatically switch to the free Starter plan. Your data will be preserved.

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees, ever. The price you see is the price you pay. All plans include SSL certificates, CDN, and standard support at no extra cost.

### Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team to apply.

### What's included in priority support?

Priority support includes faster response times (under 4 hours), direct access to senior engineers, and screen-sharing sessions when needed.
